Barcelona October 26th thru 27th
LaSalle University

Winners: Pay Your Move

Team Pay Your Move won for their application that enables Barcelona citizens to pay for their bus rides with their phones, using PayPal as the payment method. The app stood out for it's intuitive design and innovative way of notifying the user of the expiry of a ticket.

Raimon, Ricardo, and Oriol will represent Barcelona at BattleHack's World Finals in San Jose and we can't wait for another demonstration of their coding abilities.
