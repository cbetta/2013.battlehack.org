Moscow September 7th thru 8th
Digital October

Winners: Talking City

Talking City was ultimately selected for their creative approach to resolving an existing problem and an app that helps make the world a better place. The solution utilizes technology in beacons to help visually impaired people find objects in public places, such as receiving a verbal message of where the aisle with eggs, butter and milk is at the grocery store.

The team consisted of four members: Sergey Pronin, Alexander Balabna, Bayram Annakov, and Oksana Tretiakova.
