Winners: PhilanthroFeed

PhilanthroFeed enables people to help feed the homeless. Nowadays, many people don¹t carry cash around or want to ensure that the money they donate is being used for the food it was intended for. Through the app, the homeless person would create a profile via a social worker, or anyone else with Internet access. When someone wants to donate, they find the homeless person¹s profile in the app, then donate to add a credit for their account. The homeless person would then go to a participating restaurant and redeem the money for a meal.

Andrew, Kristen, Wells and Janet will be representing Washington D.C. at our World Finals in Silicon Valley.
