Winners: LateBet

LateBet is a mobile web application that allows to create dates and meetings with a friend from your social networks. Both parties commit on being on time at the arranged meeting point. If that is not the case the friend beign late has to pay 1 $ per minute being late and automatically transfers the total amount via PayPal to thepunctual participant.

Dańny, Yaniv, Maya and Yoav will be representing Israel at our Finals in the US and we're very much looking forward to their next hack!
