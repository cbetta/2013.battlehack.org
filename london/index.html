Winners: Candle Path

Team Candle Path won for their application that enables anyone to find their way home safely, using open street light data to ensure you stay out of the dark and GPS to keep make sure you stay on route. Using an in-app PayPal purchase allows anyone to buy a safe night that ensures your family and security services are notified when you deviate from your path.

Joe, Simon, Adam, and Tosin will represent London at BattleHack's World Finals in San Jose and we can't wait for another demonstration of their coding abilities.
